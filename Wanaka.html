    Wānaka

    The small little alpine town located in the south island. With views of the gorgeous blue lakes and snow capped mountains Wānaka is a beautiful town to explore! For adventure seekers, theres many ski areas surrounding the town, such as Cadrona and Treble Cone. For none skier adrenaline junkies, you could go skydiving, canyoning or even mountaineering! Wanaka is also home to many other activities for you to enjoy! Such as;
    bike tracks and hiking trails,
    kayaking on the crystal clear waters of the nearby lakes,
    lake cruises or even a plane tour to see the gorgeous scenery from up above!
    Visit the iconic Wānaka tree! a tree that as grown in the middle of the lake!
    The Wānaka lavender farm, only 20 minutes away as well to explore!

    Wānaka is a gorgeous town to explore, with so many activities to take part in, the perfect getaway for the family in the picturesque south island!
