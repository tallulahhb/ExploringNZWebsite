    Aoraki

    This is peak Aotearoa, get it? Aoraki, Mount Cook. The highest mountain in all of New Zealand, standing at a whopping 3,724 meters tall (12,217 ft)! Aoraki is stunning, the views, the snow capped mountains and all the in-betweens, This is heaven. Home to many ice glaciers and show stopping mountains, Mt Cook national park is an once in a lifetime opportunity to visit! Hooker Valley, the iconic walk to the base of Mount Cook. About a 3 hour trek, this walk is the main attraction for obvious reasons. The swing bridges and gorgeous veiws of the glaciers and rivers that run throughtout the walk, and the stunning final view of the summit of Mount Cook in all its glory.! But this national park is home to many other gems as well! The Tasman Glacier, with the blue lakes sitting below it, walk up what feels like hundreds of stairs to reach a view you won't forget! Helicopters and ski planes can take to visit all the stunning glaciers as well! with views from the planes being absolutely incredible! And if your a climber, this a great challenge and experience! The Hermitage Hotel, located in the Mt Cook town with views of mount cook right out of your window, this luxury escape is one to remember! Aoraki National Park, is a once in a lifetime opportunity to visit.
