    Queenstown

    Tāhuna

    A small town located on the shore of the iconic lake, Lake Wakatipu. With views of the dramatic snow cover Southern Alps.
    Renowned for skiing, snowboarding, and many more thrill seeking adventures Queenstown is a town for sight seeing and adventures!
    If you seek some thrill Queenstown is definitely the place for you!
    Bungee Jumping of Kawarua Gorge Bridge
    Jet boating down the Shotover and Dart Rivers!
    Skiing on the Remarkables and Coronet Peak.
    Going up the Gondolas and flying back down via the Luge!

    And if your not seeking thrill Queenstown is also for you!
    Sightseeing, drive up the mountains explore small mountain
    side towns, like Arrow-town and many more!
    Hot pools like the Onsen spa, with views of the Shotover River, for your relaxation!
    Or just exploring Queenstown, the views of the mountains are all you could ask for!
    Take a scenic cruise of Lake Whakatipu from Queenstown on board the iconic steamship TSS Earnslaw.
    Queenstown is a town for everyone, with its spectacular views and show stopping mountains, Queenstown sure is a bucket list travel destination!
