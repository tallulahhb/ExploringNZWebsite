    Rotorua

    A town known for its geothermal activity and bubbling mud pools, Rotorua may smell odd but its activities and sights to see sure aren't!
    As I said known for its Geysers, mud pools, lakes and redwood forests Rotorua is a place with lots at adventures to be had!
    Walk around and watch the many geysers erupt! Bubbling mud pools gurgle and burp. The stunning lakes with glistening waters.
    Take a walk on its treetop trail in the Whakarewarewa Forest also known as the 'Redwood Forest.' Zipline through the trees,
    soar over the amazing forest and see it from a place you have never seen before, on the Rotorua Canopy Tours!
    Cycle or mountain bike through the trees.
    Immerse yourself into the Māori culture.
    And after all of that treat yourself with a mud spa!

    Rotorua is home of Culture, Adventure, Family fun and relaxation.
    Whatever your heart desires Rotorua has you covered!
