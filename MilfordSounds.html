    Milford Sounds

    Piopiotahi

    Milford Sound is a fiord, located southwest of the south island and known for its high peaks, lush rainforests and bellowing waterfalls that plummet
    down the side of the high cliffs. Milford Sound fiord is home to wildlife such as fur seal colonies, penguins and dolphins. The Milford Discovery Centre and Underwater Observatory offers views of rare black coral and many more marine life. Boat tours are a popular way to explore Milford sounds, as it is a way to experience the sheer size of the cliffs and waterfalls bellowing down from above!

    There are many other activities to partake in as well! Such as; the Milford track a hike trail that takes you above and beyond with amazing scenery and stunning water views! Take a scenic flight! see the fiord from above! Kayaking is also a amazing way to see the fiord from down low!
    Visit the observatory, so you can take a look at what creatures live below the surface!

    Milford Sounds is a must do! Its unique scenery and gorgeous views are one you will never forget!
